# Ticket 004 - VPN Connection Failure

## Ticket Information

**Ticket ID:** HD-004
**Priority:** High
**Category:** Remote Access
**Status:** Resolved

---

## User Issue

Remote employee reported inability to connect to the company VPN while working from home.

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Verified internet connectivity.
2. Confirmed VPN credentials were correct.
3. Checked VPN client version.
4. Restarted VPN application.
5. Verified VPN server availability.
6. Reset user VPN profile.
7. Successfully established VPN connection.

---

## Root Cause

Corrupted VPN profile prevented successful authentication.

---

## Resolution

VPN profile was recreated and connection restored.
