# Ticket 003 - Outlook Not Receiving Emails

## Ticket Information

**Ticket ID:** HD-003
**Priority:** Medium
**Category:** Email Support
**Status:** Resolved

---

## User Issue

User reported Outlook was open but new emails were not appearing.

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Verified internet connectivity.
2. Confirmed Outlook was online.
3. Checked mailbox storage limits.
4. Sent a test email.
5. Restarted Outlook application.
6. Rebuilt Outlook profile.
7. Confirmed emails were syncing correctly.

---

## Root Cause

Corrupted Outlook profile prevented mailbox synchronization.

---

## Resolution

Created a new Outlook profile and restored email synchronization.
